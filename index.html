Track your PNWPS season with one tap.

PNWPS Poker Results is a small, private iOS app that helps our home poker league track seasons, games, chip bonuses, and standings using a single shared Google Sheet.

Google Sheets–backed Private
The app only reads and writes data to one specific spreadsheet owned by the league organizer. It does not browse or modify any of your other files.

What the app does

PNWPS Poker Results is built for one specific group: the PNWPS home poker league.

  • Track seasons, games, dates, and placements.
  • Automatically compute bonuses and chip counts for regular players.
  • Keep the Google Sheet and the in-app UI in sync.
  • Provide a simple in-app experience for recording game results.

Who this app is for

This app is not a public gambling service or a generic poker client. It is intended only for members of the PNWPS poker league who have been given access by the organizer.
